Yörünge (Orbit) — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Yörünge ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Yörünge does not require user registration or login. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, our app uses third-party services that may collect certain data:

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob — We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data for advertising purposes:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information on how Google handles data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Game data such as your high score and sound preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Yörünge is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads displayed are served by Google AdMob in accordance with their policies.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the app respects the App Tracking Transparency framework. You may be asked to allow or deny tracking when you first open the app. If you deny tracking, personalized ads will not be shown.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
